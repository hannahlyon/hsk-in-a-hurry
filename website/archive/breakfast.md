# 买早餐 (Mǎi Zǎocān) — Buying Breakfast at the Convenience Store

*It's a busy Monday morning. Xiao Li stops at the convenience store near the subway station to grab breakfast before work.*

---

**Speaker A (小李 / Xiǎo Lǐ):** 你好！我看一下菜单，好吗？

*(Nǐ hǎo! Wǒ kàn yí xià càidān, hǎo ma?)*
Hi! Can I take a look at the menu?

---

**Speaker B (店员 / Diànyuán):** 当然可以！你慢慢看。我们不但有面包，而且有蛋糕。

*(Dāngrán kěyǐ! Nǐ mànmàn kàn. Wǒmen bùdàn yǒu miànbāo, érqiě yǒu dàngāo.)*
Of course! Take your time. We not only have bread, but also cake.

---

**Speaker A:** 最近我总是来不及吃早饭，所以我必须快点买。

*(Zuìjìn wǒ zǒngshì láibùjí chī zǎofàn, suǒyǐ wǒ bìxū kuài diǎn mǎi.)*
Recently I always don't have time to eat breakfast, so I must buy something quickly.

---

**Speaker B:** 明白了！那你等一会儿，我马上给你介绍今天的特价。

*(Míngbai le! Nà nǐ děng yī huìr, wǒ mǎshàng gěi nǐ jièshào jīntiān de tèjià.)*
I understand! Then wait a moment — I'll introduce today's specials right away.

---

**Speaker A:** 好的。对了，冰箱里的东西都是今天的吗？

*(Hǎo de. Duì le, bīngxiāng lǐ de dōngxi dōu shì jīntiān de ma?)*
Sure. By the way, are all the things in the refrigerator from today?

---

**Speaker B:** 是的，我们每天都换。变化很大，你可以自己选。

*(Shì de, wǒmen měitiān dōu huàn. Biànhuà hěn dà, nǐ kěyǐ zìjǐ xuǎn.)*
Yes, we replace them every day. There's a big variety — you can choose yourself.

---

**Speaker A:** 那我拿两个面包吧。别人告诉我你们的面包很好吃。

*(Nà wǒ ná liǎng gè miànbāo ba. Biérén gàosù wǒ nǐmen de miànbāo hěn hǎochī.)*
Then I'll take two pieces of bread. Others told me your bread is really delicious.

---

**Speaker B:** 谢谢！你要不要尝一下这个新口味？是草莓味的。

*(Xièxiè! Nǐ yào bù yào cháng yí xià zhège xīn kǒuwèi? Shì cǎoméi wèi de.)*
Thank you! Would you like to try this new flavour? It's strawberry.

---

**Speaker A:** 好，我尝一下！嗯，真的很好吃，我很满意！那我再买一个蛋糕。

*(Hǎo, wǒ cháng yí xià! Ń, zhēn de hěn hǎochī, wǒ hěn mǎnyì! Nà wǒ zài mǎi yī gè dàngāo.)*
OK, I'll try it! Mmm, it really is delicious — I'm very satisfied! Then I'll also buy a cake.

---

**Speaker B:** 好主意！你打算怎么去上班？坐地铁吗？

*(Hǎo zhúyì! Nǐ dǎsuàn zěnme qù shàngbān? Zuò dìtiě ma?)*
Good idea! How do you plan to get to work? By subway?

---

**Speaker A:** 对，我骑自行车去地铁站，然后坐地铁。我很担心迟到。

*(Duì, wǒ qí zìxíngchē qù dìtiě zhàn, ránhòu zuò dìtiě. Wǒ hěn dānxīn chídào.)*
Right, I ride my bicycle to the subway station, then take the subway. I'm really worried about being late.

---

**Speaker B:** 那注意安全！这些东西我帮你包好。

*(Nà zhùyì ānquán! Zhèxiē dōngxi wǒ bāng nǐ bāo hǎo.)*
Then take care! Let me wrap these things up for you.

---

**Speaker A:** 太好了！对了，超市里也有面包卖吗？我问是因为有时候我去超市买别的东西。

*(Tài hǎo le! Duì le, chāoshì lǐ yě yǒu miànbāo mài ma? Wǒ wèn shì yīnwèi yǒu shíhou wǒ qù chāoshì mǎi biéde dōngxi.)*
That's great! By the way, do supermarkets also sell bread? I'm asking because I sometimes go to the supermarket to buy other things.

---

**Speaker B:** 有，但是我们便利店的质量比较好，而且你离这里更近！一会儿你就知道了，吃了以后你最后还是会来这里买！

*(Yǒu, dànshì wǒmen biànlì diàn de zhìliàng bǐjiào hǎo, érqiě nǐ lí zhèlǐ gèng jìn! Yīhuìr nǐ jiù zhīdào le, chī le yǐhòu nǐ zuìhòu háishì huì lái zhèlǐ mǎi!)*
Yes, but our convenience store's quality is comparatively better, and you're closer to here! In a short while you'll know — after eating it, you'll end up coming back here to buy more!

---

**Speaker A:** 哈哈，好，我相信你！谢谢，再见！

*(Hāhā, hǎo, wǒ xiāngxìn nǐ! Xièxiè, zàijiàn!)*
Haha, OK, I believe you! Thank you, goodbye!

---

## Key Phrases

- **看一下菜单** *(kàn yí xià càidān)* — "look once at the menu" — uses 一下 to indicate briefly attempting an action (just having a quick look)

- **等一会儿** *(děng yīhuìr)* — "wait a short while" — uses 一会儿 as a time adverbial meaning a short duration

- **尝一下** *(cháng yí xià)* — "try/taste once" — uses 一下 to indicate trying something once, a common polite invitation

- **一会儿你就知道了** *(yīhuìr nǐ jiù zhīdào le)* — "in a short while you will know" — 一会儿 used as a time adverbial at the start of a sentence to mean "soon / in a moment"
